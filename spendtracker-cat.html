Welcome 👋

Welcome to SpendTracker
SpendTracker helps you keep on top of all your recurring expenses — subscriptions, insurance, memberships, phone plans, and anything else that renews regularly.
📋Track every renewal date so nothing sneaks up on you
📅See the whole year on the calendar — with coloured dots for each expense
📊Breakdown charts show where your money is going by category and month
💾Your data stays on your device — export a backup any time
Step 1 of 2
➕
Adding a recurring expense
Tap + Recurring Spend to add an entry. You'll fill in a few details:
🏷Service name — e.g. Netflix, Car Insurance, Costco
💰Cost & billing cycle — monthly, quarterly, every 6 months, annual, or weekly
📆Next renewal date — when it's next due to charge
💳Payment method — AMEX, Visa, Mastercard, or Direct Deposit
🎨Colour & icon — to make it easy to spot on the calendar
You're all set ✨
🚀
Ready to add your first entry
A few handy things to know as you get started:
⏸You can pause or cancel an entry without deleting it — useful for things that are temporarily stopped
↻The renew button appears on a card when something is due — one tap advances it by one cycle
⧉Duplicate an entry to quickly add a similar expense
💾Use Export to back up your data, and Import to restore it
